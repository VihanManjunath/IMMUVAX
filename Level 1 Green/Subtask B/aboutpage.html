Our Mission:

Our mission is to empower individuals and communities with accurate, unbiased vaccination data. We aim to provide clear insights into immunisation trends, coverage, and outcomes across countries, regions, and populations. By offering both high-level summaries and detailed analysis, we support informed decision-making for researchers, policymakers, healthcare professionals, students, and the public. Through accessible and respectful presentation of data, we strive to promote global awareness and understanding of preventable diseases.

How you can use our platform?

Users can explore global vaccination and infection data through interactive filters by country, region, year, economic status, and disease type. The website provides high-level summaries as well as tools for deep analysis, such as comparing vaccination targets, tracking infection trends, and identifying progress toward herd immunity. Researchers, policymakers, healthcare students, and the public can use it to gain reliable insights and support evidence-based decisions. By presenting data in a clear, unbiased way, the site empowers users to better understand the impact of immunisation worldwide.
